Registration Notes

  • Student fee is applicable only with valid identity proof.
  • The registration fee includes publication charges.
  • For on-site participants, it includes registration kit and fooding charges (high tea, working lunch, and dinner during conference days).
  • Listeners are not entitled to a full conference kit.
  • Extra page charges are Rs. 1180 per page (including 18% GST).
  • A maximum of 17 pages is allowed per paper, including extra pages.
