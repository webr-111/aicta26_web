Call for Papers

The AICTA 2026 engages academicians, researchers, practitioners, educational developers, and industry leaders to address current challenges and advances in Artificial Intelligence. AICTA 2026 will take place in hybrid mode on 02-03 October 2026 in Silchar, India with the conference theme AI for next-generation Computing.

Original contributions are invited from prospective authors with interests in the following tracks and related application areas.

Best Paper Awards

The Best Paper Awards will be given to the authors of registered and presented papers in AICTA 2026. The Best Paper Awards will be selected based on scientific contribution, writing/presentation style, reviewers' comments, etc. The "Best Paper Awards" will be given for each track.

Call for Special Session

We invite proposals from academicians, technologists, experts and industry persons for special sessions in the area of conferences. Please send your proposal to aicta2026@nits.ac.in.
